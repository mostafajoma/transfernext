Know-Your-Customer (KYC)

Due to industry restrictions, alternative asset managers are only allowed to source capital from corporations, institutional investors, and accredited investors. Using blockchain, we can store customer information in a secure, decentralized repository, and ensure that information on clients is validated in a trustworthy manner.

Blockchain-Based Back & Middle Office Services

Especially for hedge funds trading in public markets, post-crisis regulation has mandated greater transparency into the trade process to ensure best execution. By posting trade details to a blockchain, these could be communicated to a regulator in a streamlined and secure manner on a live-basis, reducing the need for periodic audits and reports. This is but one of the many use cases blockchain can solve for in the back and middle office of asset managers.
